Research Summary

Our lab focuses on Deep learning and Data mining to transfom large datasets into useful knowledge and developing different explainability methods. Our current emphasis is on extracting causal entities and relations between words using huge amount of text data and utilize Convolution methods to identify specific regions using image data.

Research Projects


Causality Extraction






This project aims at classification of causal sentences, extracting cause and effect entities from causal sentences and determining strength of relationship between cause and effect entity. This project has received the funding of National Science Foundation.

HWC hotspots


Human wildlife conflicts (HWC) are always a threat to food, shelter, habitat loss and even life. In this project we are identifying the HWC hotspots according to transformation of land use by humans invading the forest areas of wildlife.

Sjogren's Syndrome


Sjogren's syndrome is a chronic autoimmune disorder identified by its two most common symptoms - dry eyes and a dry mouth. Our project has annotated sentences extracted from abstracts of various papers. The project goal is to diagnose Sjogren's syndrome early by classifying each word in a sentence into one of the four categories: 1. Risk Factors 2. Associated Conditions 3. Diagnostics tests 4. Signs and Symptoms. The results from this model will help diagnose the patients with Sjogren's syndrome.
