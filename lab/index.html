Data Science Lab- IUPUI

Data Science Lab comes under School of Informatics and Computing at Indiana University-Purdue University-Indianapolis. This lab focuses on Deep learning and Data mining to transfom large datasets into useful knowledge and developing different explainability methods. We specialize in exploring unstructured text data to find relationship between different words in text, dealing with medical text, creating classification methods using image data to find specific regions.
