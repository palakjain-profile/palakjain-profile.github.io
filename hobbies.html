For me, Dance is something which makes me happy and full of positive energy. I liked Dancing since the childhood but in the beginning, I had stage fear. With my family support, gradually I started to enjoy being on stage and participated in many Interschool and Intercollege Dance competition. In 2018, I went for Intensive Training Program at Nritya Shakti Studio by Shakti Mohan, Mumbai. Also attended Urban Dance Camp in Mumbai. I did Dance Choreography in various social and cultural events. Dance helps to increase my self esteem and encourages me to learn and try something new. It is like mental and physical relaxation to me.
